### Common Challenges & Solutions
<Accordion title="Data Migration Complexity">
**Challenge**: Legacy systems with inconsistent data formats and quality issues

**Solution**: Comprehensive data mapping and cleansing process with iterative validation and testing
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="User Adoption Resistance">
**Challenge**: Staff reluctance to change established workflows and processes

**Solution**: Early user involvement, comprehensive training programs, and clear communication of personal benefits
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Integration Technical Issues">
**Challenge**: Unexpected technical challenges with legacy system connectivity

**Solution**: Thorough technical assessment, contingency planning, and phased integration approach
</Accordion>

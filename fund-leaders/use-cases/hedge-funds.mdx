# Hedge Fund Use Cases

## Overview

Hedge funds operate in complex, fast-moving markets requiring sophisticated risk management and performance attribution capabilities. Amos provides institutional-grade solutions for hedge fund operations.

## Key Use Cases

### Risk Management & Monitoring
- Real-time portfolio risk analytics
- Automated compliance monitoring
- Stress testing and scenario analysis

### Performance Attribution
- Multi-factor performance attribution analysis
- Benchmark comparison and tracking
- Alpha generation measurement and reporting

### Regulatory Reporting
- Automated regulatory filing preparation
- Compliance monitoring and alerting
- Audit trail documentation and management

## Specific Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Daily Risk Reporting
**Challenge**: Manual risk calculations taking 4+ hours daily

**Solution**: Automated risk analytics with real-time monitoring

**Outcome**: Risk reporting completed in under 30 minutes with enhanced accuracy

### Scenario 2: Regulatory Compliance
**Challenge**: Complex regulatory requirements across multiple jurisdictions

**Solution**: Automated compliance monitoring and reporting workflows

**Outcome**: 95% reduction in compliance preparation time with improved accuracy
